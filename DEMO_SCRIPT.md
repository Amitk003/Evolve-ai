# Demo Script — Evolve.ai

**Duration:** ~3 minutes 30 seconds

**Judging criteria:** Presentation, Originality, Social Value, UI/UX

---

## Act 1: The Fear (0:00 — 0:30)

> *Presenter stands at laptop, screen projected.*

"We've all seen the headlines. 'AI is coming for your job.' 'Accountants replaced by algorithms.' '60% of jobs will be automated.'

We asked ourselves a different question: What if a finance app didn't just track your past spending, but funded your future?

Meet **Evolve.ai**."

**[Click to focus on the input screen]**

---

## Act 2: The Input (0:30 — 1:00)

> *Type "Junior Accountant" in the search bar. Select from dropdown.*

"Let's look at Alex's reality. Alex is 28, a Junior Accountant making $4,000 a month. Every day she hears that AI is coming for her job. She's paralyzed — doesn't know what to learn or how to pay for it."

**[Salary auto-fills. Click "Run My Evolution".]**

---

## Act 3: The Pivot (1:00 — 2:00)

> *Timeline page appears with animated vertical timeline.*

"Here's what most people get wrong. AI isn't going to fire Alex. But an accountant using AI will."

**[Point to each year as it appears]**

"In 2024, 70% of her job is manual data entry. By 2025, AI handles 40% of tasks — Alex shifts to analysis. By 2027, she's not entering data at all. She's an **AI Anomaly Auditor & Strategic Advisor** — a $55,000/year role."

"See the two skills she needs? Prompt Engineering for Auditing and Python for Data Validation. Specific, learnable, achievable."

**[Click "See the Financial Impact"]**

---

## Act 4: The Financials (2:00 — 2:45)

> *Split-screen appears with dramatic animation.*

"This is the moment. On the left — the Static Path. Salary stays flat. Redundancy risk: high.

On the right — the **Evolved Path**. Alex's salary jumps 38% to over $55,000.

But here's the catch: evolution requires capital. Those skills cost money. Courses, certificates, time."

**[Point to the budget allocator]**

---

## Act 5: The Climax (2:45 — 3:30)

> *Click "Allocate to Evolve Fund".*

"Evolve.ai doesn't just tell you to learn Python. It finds the money to pay for it.

We analyzed Alex's spending and found $50 a month sitting in unused subscriptions and dining out. With one click, we reallocate that into a locked **Evolve Fund**.

$50 a month. $600 over 12 months. That's a Python certificate. That's a Prompt Engineering bootcamp.

Alex isn't just budgeting anymore. Alex is **buying her future**."

---

## Outro (3:30+)

"So we shift the narrative. From **AI anxiety** to **AI empowerment**.

Evolve.ai proves that the worker who adapts — with a financial roadmap and micro-budgeting — doesn't just survive AI. They thrive because of it.

Don't predict the end of your career. **Finance its evolution.** "

**[Smile. Wait for questions.]**

---

## Technical Notes for Presenter

- **Demo reliability:** All job profiles are hardcoded. No API calls needed.
- **Backup plan:** If pages don't load, refresh and re-enter "Junior Accountant".
- **Animations are triggered once:** If you go back, you may need to refresh.
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 recommended. Use browser in fullscreen (F11).
